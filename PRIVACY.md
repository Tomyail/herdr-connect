# Privacy Policy

[简体中文](docs/zh-CN/PRIVACY.md)

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Herdr Connect is an open-source companion app that discovers and connects to a Herdr daemon on the same local network.

## Data handled by the app

Herdr Connect may temporarily process and display information provided by a daemon you control, including installation details, Agent status and activity history, and messages you choose to send. This information is exchanged directly between your device and the daemon on your local network.

## Data collection

The Herdr Connect project does not operate an account system or cloud service and does not intentionally collect, sell, or share personal data. The current MVP does not send local daemon data to project-operated servers.

When you install a beta through TestFlight, Apple may collect diagnostics, crash reports, and feedback under Apple's own privacy policies and TestFlight terms.

## Local network access

The app requests Local Network permission to discover the Bonjour service `_herdr-connect._tcp` and connect to a daemon on your network. You can revoke this permission in iOS Settings.

## Security

The current MVP is intended for trusted local networks. It does not provide remote connectivity. You are responsible for the daemon and network to which you connect.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as Herdr Connect evolves. Material changes will be published in this repository.
